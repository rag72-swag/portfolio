TEXTURE

Scratched, Crushed, and Layered

What happens when images lose fidelity? They gain texture. Blocks of color replace faces, and their edges become static. This breakdown of form tells a new story... One with interference and emotion baked in.

Think of an old VHS, playing back a memory you don’t quite remember. Distortion becomes the filter of time. The image is damaged, but the feeling survives on and on.
